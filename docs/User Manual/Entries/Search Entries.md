*   **How to Search:** Simply type into the search field at the top of the ledger content. The list will filter in real-time.
*   **What is searched:** The search scans through the **ID**, the **Genre**, and the **Data** field of every entry.
*   **Quick Access:** You can instantly jump focus to the search bar by pressing **`Alt + F`**.

The search is case-insensitive, making it easy to find entries even if you are unsure of the exact casing used.
