1.  Click the **Add Entry** button (the `+` icon) in the action toolbar, or press **`Alt + E`**.
2.  **Select a Genre:** Choose a category from the dropdown menu (e.g., *commit*, *push*, *add*). This helps keep your logs consistent.
3.  **Enter Data:** Type your note or information into the Data field.
4.  Click **Add**. The entry is instantly encrypted and saved to the ledger.
