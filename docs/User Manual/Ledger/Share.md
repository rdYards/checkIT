You can share an entire ledger with other CheckIT users on the same local area network (LAN).

1.  Open the ledger you wish to share.
2.  Press **`Ctrl + E`**.
3.  The **Share with Peer** dialog will appear, listing all discovered CheckIT users on your network.
4.  Select the peer you want to send the ledger to.
5.  Click **Send**.

The ledger is encrypted using X25519 and AES-256-GCM specifically for the recipient, ensuring that only the intended peer can decrypt and access the contents.
