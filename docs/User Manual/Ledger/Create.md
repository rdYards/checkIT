When you first open CheckIT, you can start a brand-new secure vault.
1.  Click the **New Ledger** icon in the main window, or press **`Ctrl + N`**.
2.  A dialog will appear. Enter a **Title** (the name of your vault) and a **Description** (a brief note about what this ledger is for).
3.  **Set a Password:** This is the most important step. Choose a strong password. 
    * **WARNING:** There is no "Forgot Password" feature. If you lose this password, your data is lost forever.
4.  Click **Create**. Your new ledger will appear in the sidebar.
